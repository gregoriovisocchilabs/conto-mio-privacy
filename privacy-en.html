Privacy Policy - Conto Mio

Last updated: 2026-02-16

1. Data Controller

The data controller is Gregorio Visocchi Labs.
Contact: gregoriovisocchilabs@gmail.com

2. App Scope and Nature

Conto Mio is an app for creating counters, tracking counted events, and reviewing value history. The app does not require a user account and does not register users.

3. What Data We Process

3.1 Data Stored Locally (on your device)

  • counter data (e.g., name, current value, step, settings, and status flags),
  • counter event history (e.g., increment/decrement/reset with timestamps),
  • app settings (e.g., language, theme, sound, vibration, ad preferences).

Data is stored locally, mainly in SQLite and system preferences (SharedPreferences).

3.2 Data Import and Export

The app allows exporting data to a JSON file and importing data from a user-selected JSON file. These operations are performed locally on your device and do not upload your data to our servers.

3.3 Notifications

The app can display a persistent notification for quick counting actions from the system notification area. On Android 13+, POST_NOTIFICATIONS permission may be required.

3.4 Advertising - Google AdMob

The app uses Google AdMob. In connection with ad delivery, Google may process, among others:

  • device advertising identifier,
  • IP address,
  • technical device and operating system data,
  • information about ad interactions.

The app includes ad preference settings (personalized / non-personalized ads).

Details: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3.5 Android System Backups

If Android system backup is enabled on your device, the operating system may back up local app data (such as database and settings) to your account backup or during device-to-device transfer.

4. Processing Purposes and Legal Bases (GDPR)

  • Service delivery (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR): processing local data required for app functionality.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR): settings related to ad personalization.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR): app maintenance through ad monetization.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell user data. Data recipients may include:

  • Google (for AdMob advertising),
  • operating system / backup service provider, if system backups are enabled by the user.

6. Transfers Outside the EEA

Google may process data outside the European Economic Area. Transfers are carried out in accordance with mechanisms required by applicable law.

7. Retention Period

  • local app data: until deleted by the user, overwritten by import, or app uninstallation,
  • exported JSON files: until deleted by the user,
  • ad-related data: according to Google policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • access your data,
  • rectify your data,
  • erase your data,
  • restrict processing,
  • data portability (for local data),
  • object to processing,
  • withdraw consent (where consent is the legal basis).

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with a competent data protection supervisory authority.

9. Security

We apply data minimization: the app works locally and does not operate our own backend for user data storage. We recommend securing your device (screen lock) and handling exported files carefully.

10. Children

The app is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided data unintentionally, please contact us.

11. Policy Changes

Any changes to this policy will be published in this document with an updated date.
